Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Why does the map look different from standard maps?

A: Jianghu uses a custom "Cyberpunk" map style powered by OpenStreetMap data to highlight the unique urban structure of Chongqing.

Q: Can I use the app offline?

A: Yes! Once you view a location, its details are cached on your device. You can access them later even without an internet connection.
