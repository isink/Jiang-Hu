FREQUENTLY ASKED QUERIES

1. My GPS signal is drifting?

Chongqing is an 8D city with extreme vertical interference. Ensure you have a clear view of the sky. We use WGS-84 correction, but skyscrapers may still cause signal bounces.

2. NetRunner (Premium) status not showing?

Go to your ID page -> Initialize Uplink -> Click "Restore Purchases". This will sync your Google Play credentials with RevenueCat.

3. How to report a map error?

Select the POI on the map and tap "REPORT ISSUE". Our data-runners will verify the location manually.
