Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-12-15

Welcome to Jianghu ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Data

Our App requires access to your device's location to provide core features, including:

  • Displaying your current position on the cyberpunk map.
  • Calculating distance to nearby landmarks and Points of Interest (POIs).
  • Tracking your movement in "Follow Mode" for navigation assistance.

Important: Location data is processed locally on your device for navigation purposes. We do not track, store history of, or sell your location data to third parties.

1.2 User Accounts (Optional)

If you choose to create an account, we store:

  • Email Address: Used for authentication and password recovery.
  • Nickname & Avatar: Publicly displayed when you post comments.

We use Supabase as our backend service provider to securely store this data.

1.3 Device & Crash Data

We automatically collect certain data to help us identify and fix bugs, including:

  • Device model, OS version, and App version.
  • Stack traces and error logs when the App crashes.

This data is anonymous and does not personally identify you.

1.4 User-Generated Content

Any comments, ratings, or issue reports you submit are stored publicly and associated with your nickname.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely to:

  • Provide and improve the App's functionality.
  • Authenticate users and secure user accounts.
  • Display community-driven content (comments/reviews).
  • Monitor app stability and fix technical issues.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For database and authentication services.
  • Sentry: For real-time error tracking and performance monitoring.
  • Expo: For app updates and infrastructure.
  • OpenStreetMap (Overpass API): To fetch map data.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (including Row Level Security) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
