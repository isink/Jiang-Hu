SYSTEM PROTOCOL: PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: December 22, 2025

1. Data Collection & Usage (Neural Link)

Jianghu collects specific data points to ensure your navigation through the 8D City is seamless:

  • Geolocation Data (Location): We access your precise location ONLY while the app is in use to display your position on the map, calculate distances to landmarks, and provide navigation. This data is processed locally and is NOT stored on our servers history.
  • Identity Data (Account): When you register via Supabase, we collect your email address, nickname, and authentication tokens to manage your user profile and "Memory Stash" (Favorites).
  • Purchase History (IAP): We use RevenueCat to process in-app subscriptions and purchases. We store your purchase entitlement status to unlock premium features. We DO NOT store your credit card details; financial transactions are handled entirely by Google Play.
  • Device Telemetry: We use Sentry to collect anonymous crash logs and device performance metrics to fix bugs and maintain system integrity.

2. Third-Party Sub-processors

We transmit minimal necessary data to the following secure nodes:

  • Supabase: For secure user authentication and database storage.
  • RevenueCat: For verifying subscription status and handling Google Play transactions.
  • Sentry: For real-time error monitoring.
  • OpenStreetMap: Map data rendering (no personal data sent).

3. Data Security

Your data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS protocols. We do not sell your personal data to third parties or advertisers.

4. Account Deletion & User Rights (Critical)

You have full control over your data. You may request the complete deletion of your account and all associated data (including email and favorites) at any time by:

  • Using the "Delete Account" button within the App Settings.
  • Sending a request via email to our support terminal: handwanly@gmail.com

Upon request, your data will be permanently purged from our Supabase servers within 30 days.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

6. Contact

For privacy inquiries or data deletion requests, initiate a secure uplink via: handwanly@gmail.com
